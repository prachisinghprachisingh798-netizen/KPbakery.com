Our story

Two names, one oven, and a lot of early mornings.

KP Bakery began in 2014 when Kavita and Puneet turned their home kitchen into a two-table storefront. Kavita handled the sourdough starter she'd been feeding since university; Puneet built the cases and figured out the ovens. The initials stuck as the name.

We still mill in small batches, proof overnight, and shape everything by hand before the first delivery goes out. Nothing sits under a heat lamp — what's on the shelf is what came out of the oven that morning.
